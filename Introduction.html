Lesson Recap: Introduction to Connect
Connect is a single integrated IT solution used by multiple forces to facilitate the sharing of a single data store.
Benefits - Better access to improved quality, real-time information
- Easier to support cross-border investigations
- Elimination of physical storage
- Improved investigation linkage and analysis
- Improved notification on special POLE objects (markers and flags)
- Forces the activation of JCF Email
- Enforcement of accountability at all levels
- Standardized information collection
- Enforcement of adherence to timeline
- Improved investigative communication
- Instant submission of reported incidents
- Elimination of misplacement/lost documents
- Equitable distribution/allocation of investigations
- Easier reassignment of investigations
- Removal of duplication of efforts
- Reduction of human errors
- Golden Nominal: Any POLE object that is linked to multiple investigations

Connect Roles

Level One: Crime Officer
Level Two: Divisional Detective Inspectors
Level Three: Registry
Level Four: Station Commanders
Level Five: Corporals/Sergeants and Core Users

Example: Firearm Linked to Multiple Investigations

A firearm can be an example of a POLE object that is linked to multiple investigations. If the same firearm is used in two different investigations, it would be considered a Golden Nominal.
