Exam

Two men who were brothers, stole a mobile phone from a woman who was walking down a street. The younger brother then took her car keys. A teenage man, who happened to witness the incident, promptly informed the police. Despite this, the brothers managed to drive away in the woman's car, which they had taken without her consent."

Create an investigation in Conenct with the information provided. Create the investigation first with the three mandatory cards, submit the investigation, and then update it by adding the rest of the appropriate cards. (30 points)
  A witness statement should be GENERATED. (5 points)
  UPLOAD a Police Statement Create two new version of the Police statement. (5 points)
  A CR-10 form needs to be generated and link to victim. (10 points)
  Warning markers should be place on the vehicle and both suspects. (10 points)
  One of the suspect is now deceased, the investigation will need to be updated to reflect this information. (10 points)
  A photograph of the victim's car and stolen property needs to be uploaded to the investigation. (10 points)
  A message needs to be sent to USERM with the investigation included as a reference. The subject of the message should be "[Your Name] Investigation." The body of the message should state "Please see the attached record." (10 points)
  Create an action for USERM requesting that they to obtain a further statement from the witness (10 points).
