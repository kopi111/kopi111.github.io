Two men, who are cousins, snatched a diamond necklace from a man while he was jogging in a park. The older cousin then assaulted the victim, causing severe head injuries that led to his death, and stole his motor car, which was parked in a nearby parking lot. A college student who witnessed the incident called the police and the cousins were apprehended.
The victim is 35 years old. The witness is a 20 years old female.
1. Create an investigation in Connect and utilize the "Deceased" tab on the relevant person card(s). You are free to supply information such as DOB, addresses, etc., as necessary to complete the task (20 points).
2. Generate a statement (5 points).
3. Generate a CR-10 (5 points).
4. Upload a photograph of the victim's motor car and stolen property (10 points).
5. Send an action to USERM, instructing them to collect CCTV footage from the incident location (10 points).
6. Send a message to USERM with the investigation included as a reference. The subject of the message should be "[Your Name] Investigation." The body of the message should state, "Please see the attached record." (5 points).
7. Record an enquiry log on the investigation (5 points).
8. Register USERM as an interested party (5 points).
9. Perform an investigative review on the investigation then assign it Cons 1A 1822 Area2 (10 points).
10. One of the suspects is bailed so you are to update their status accordingly (5 points.)
11. The motor vehicle was recovered so you are to update the necessary vehicle card(s).
